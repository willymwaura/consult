Harness Data, Analytics & Technology to Improve Performance

Robust data analytics capabilities and well-designed systems are essential to becoming more efficient and delivering better care. That’s why so many organizations turn to the expert healthcare technology consultants at Freed..

We ensure that investments in data management, analytics and technology deliver the best possible outcomes. And we work on all aspects of technology, from devising system strategies to extracting new insights from data and implementing complex solutions. In addition, to ensure that organizations take the smartest approach to tackling changes and challenges, we approach projects with a bigger lens that incorporates integration, optimization, management and governance.

Areas of Focus

- Data Strategy, Analytics and Management

To improve operations and care delivery while making better business and clinical decisions, you have to unlock the secrets in your data. That’s why leading healthcare organizations have entrusted Freed with more than 50 data analytics projects over the last five years. Our healthcare analytics consultants can help you optimize data flow, data assets and data sharing through robust data-management standards. We can give you a clear vision and strategy for your enterprise data management. And we can deploy the analytics it takes to deliver the insights you need.
