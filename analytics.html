    Healthcare Analytics & Technology Consulting

    Advance your business operations and care delivery through data insights and technology enablement.

    Harness Data, Analytics & Technology to Improve Performance

    Robust data analytics capabilities and well-designed systems are essential to becoming more efficient and delivering better care. That’s why so many organizations turn to the expert healthcare technology consultants at Freed. We ensure that investments in data management, analytics and technology deliver the best possible outcomes. And we work on all aspects of technology, from devising system strategies to extracting new insights from data and implementing complex solutions. In addition, to ensure that organizations take the smartest approach to tackling changes and challenges, we approach projects with a bigger lens that incorporates integration, optimization, management and governance.

    Areas of focus

    - Data stratergy ,Analytics and management

    To improve operations and care delivery while making better business and clinical decisions, you have to unlock the secrets in your data. That’s why leading healthcare organizations have entrusted Freed with more than 50 data analytics projects over the last five years. Our healthcare analytics consultants can help you optimize data flow, data assets and data sharing through robust data-management standards. We can give you a clear vision and strategy for your enterprise data management. And we can deploy the analytics it takes to deliver the insights you need. Alignment of data capabilities with business needs Advancing analytics and building business intelligence capabilities Data governance assessment, strategy and implementation

    + System Implementation and optimization

    When it comes to helping you manage complex system implementations and improve the way your existing technology supports emerging business needs, we have an extensive track record of success. During implementation planning, we always factor in operational readiness, change management, and organizational impact to achieve stakeholder buy-in. We guide you through each step of implementation, beginning with key decision-maker engagement and system design. And we conduct rigorous testing, training and transition-to-production with “at the elbow” support to ensure a smooth go-live. Trained in Lean and Agile methodologies, our healthcare technology consultants work closely with your stakeholders to serve as a bridge for success between your business teams and technology partners. Technology design, implementation and optimization Technology system integrations Solution go-live planning and support

    + Technology Strategy

    Lean in or get left behind. You need well-thought-out strategies to take full advantage of new technologies and healthcare digital transformation initiatives. Especially when it comes to minimizing risk and maximizing reward. Freed’s healthcare technology consultants can help you evaluate your existing technology, advance your business objectives, eliminate redundancy or excess costs stemming from disparate systems, and identify integrations that boost optimization. In addition, we’re a completely agnostic partner that can evaluate complex vendor capabilities, and recommend innovative technologies that will improve your clinical and business excellence. Technology portfolio planning and road map development Technology solution assessment and selection Solution benefits analysis and realization planning
