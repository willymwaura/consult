Healthcare Operations Consulting

Optimize key aspects of your care delivery and health plan operations.

Drive lasting change for the better.

Healthcare never stands still. Neither do our clients. They turn to Freed’s expert healthcare operations consultants to help them grow, optimize, evolve, improve, and keep up with ever-changing challenges. Freed can help you drive improvement opportunities ranging from care management to facilities planning and from member administration to patient experience and outcomes — and more.

At Freed, our healthcare operations consultants are steeped in applying Lean, Six-Sigma, and Agile methodologies. We work closely alongside all stakeholders to understand your business needs and processes. We dig into your challenges and partner with the frontline professionals managing day-to-day work. And we build solutions that achieve significant, lasting improvement.

Areas of Focus

- Bussiness Operations and Care Delivery Improvement

Freed helps you tackle the most complex operational challenges, whether that’s creating a better experience for patients and members, improving access to services, or addressing escalating costs.

  • Operational assessment, design, and implementation
  • Consumer experience research and analysis
  • Operating model, workflow redesign, and supporting technology improvements
  • Performance measurement development and monitoring

+ Care Management

Now, more than ever, integrated delivery systems and health plans are adjusting care management practices to address the needs of patients across the continuum of care and to achieve the best possible outcomes.

  • Assessment of the end-to-end care continuum and associated recommendations
  • Future state organizational model development
  • Implementation support for technology solutions
  • Program development for end-of-life programs
  • Care management Key Performance Indicator (KPI) development

+ Healthcare Facility Planning and execution

Making sure patients get the right care in the right place at the right time can mean consolidating, moving, or building facilities. Freed has the expertise you need to maximize efficiency and deliver the best possible patient experiences.

  • Demand, capacity, and space planning
  • Site selection
  • Workflow redesign, including Lean process optimization
  • Staffing model optimization
  • Device management and technical integration
  • Communication planning, move logistics, and go-live support
