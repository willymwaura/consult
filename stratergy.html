    Healthcare Strategy Consulting

    Put strategic plans into action that meet and exceed your performance goals.

    Implement healthcare strategies that deliver powerful results.

    With Freed’s healthcare strategy consulting services, you won’t get big, overreaching plans that sit on a shelf. Instead, you’ll receive real-world strategies with realistic, achievable approaches and plans to exceed your goals. And Freed is all in for the hard part — making strategy a reality

    Areas of focus

    - Growth Strategies and Execution

    As part of any initiative, Freed works with you and your teams to successfully frame and manage your strategic growth initiatives. Freed knows how to drive strategic initiatives, such as introducing new products and lines of business, market and service line expansion, and new community and business partnerships. We also know how to keep you focused on what’s important in order to reach your stated goals. Visioning sessions and workshops Business case development and value quantification Operating model development Capacity and facilities planning Performance measurement Program management utilizing Agile principles

    Dramatic change initiatives continue to be in high demand throughout the healthcare industry. Most organizations have a keen sense of what they want to do. Where they often stumble is in the ongoing decision-making required to successfully manage a portfolio of enterprise-wide, strategic initiatives. That’s why our Freed teams work with the senior leadership of our clients to establish an operating structure.
