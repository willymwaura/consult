    Health Equity Consulting

    Take on health inequities with confidence — and achieve measurable impact toward eliminating disparities.

    Let’s build a more equitable healthcare ecosystem.

    Inequities in healthcare cost organizations $320 billion annually. Despite heavy regulation to address disparities in care, health equity officers across California are under-resourced, underfunded and under immense pressure. That’s why many organizations turn to the health equity consultants with Freed Associates. We’re experts on tackling the tough stuff and making solutions stick. So, we can help you work toward an ecosystem where the communities and individuals your organization serves have an opportunity to realize their best health, regardless of background or circumstance. Together, we can begin healing healthcare by addressing the underlying factors that contribute to disparities in health outcomes — and create a more fair and just healthcare landscape for everyone..

    Areas of focus

    - Cultural Transformation to Attain Health Equity

    Becoming an equitable healthcare organization requires alignment on a shared vision and a culture of equity. At Freed, we can meet you where you are in your health equity journey, from establishing your organization-wide vision to defining what constitutes success to aligning your culture with desired outcomes to implementing extensive change management. Discover how Freed can develop a custom plan that helps you: Engage leadership and establish health equity champions to foster a sense of ownership for improving equity throughout your organization Align your operational structures and incentives to actively support equity goals Quantify the social and financial impact of your equity efforts Develop a shared language and a safe environment for engaging in difficult conversations about racial and social inequities that are essential to addressing inequitable health outcomes for your members, patients and employees

    + Data insights for Health Equity

    Health equity improvement hinges upon data. It’s essential to identify inequities among the populations you serve, design and implement targeted programs to address inequities, track the improvement of health outcomes and comply with regulatory reporting requirements. Freed can help you to address the challenges of effectively gathering accurate, robust, disaggregated, self-reported data so that you can: Pinpoint populations that have inequitable health outcomes and develop targeted interventions Build programs that enhance trust between patients/members and data collectors to increase self-reporting Develop governance and consistency around data capture, usage and reporting Accommodate varying data requirements across lines of business and geographical locations Define KPIs to monitor the impact of your efforts on health outcomes

    + Health Equity Compliance

    Federal and state regulators, as well as purchasers, are passing numerous access, quality, experience and affordability-related health equity policies that require effort to interpret and implement. Freed can help you review requirements, assess your internal policies and practices, identify required actions and implement changes. We can work with you to: Interpret new regulations and perform impact analysis Complete compliance readiness assessments and gap analysis Design and implement processes, staffing and system changes to support new regulations Develop and optimize the organizational operating model to meet health equity goals Create and execute corrective action plans
